Analysis of Tweets from @WeRateDogs Twitter Account¶

WeRateDogs is a Twitter page that regularly shares pictures of dogs along with a catchy description and often a rating out of 10 for the dog in the picture, sometimes exceeding 10.

This analysis answers four questions by exploring changes in tweets favorites, retweets, and ratings over time. They are:

  • Is there a relationship between rating numerator and favorite count?
  • Is there a relationship between rating numerator and retweet count?
  • Is there a relationship between favorite count and retweet count?
  • Which are the popular dog breeds?

Four different visualizations will be used to answer these questions.
